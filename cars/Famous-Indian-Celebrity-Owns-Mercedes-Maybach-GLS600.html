Famous Indian Celebrity who owns Mercedes-Maybach GLS600

In the recent time, Mercedes Maybach GLS600 has become one of the most favorite SUVs from Bollywood celebrity's garages. The car always used to be Range Rover Vogue which is used by most Bollywood stars including Salaman Khan, Janhavi Kapoor, and Vicky Kaushal.

Maybach GLS600 has taken all attention after many purchases from Indian celebrities.

Back in the day when maharajas ruled they had particular transport elephants and they were comfortable, large could go anywhere. The Maybach GLS is something like that and No doubt it is King Size SUV.

Many famous personalities have opted to buy the new luxury SUV. This is because GLS600 offers the luxury and comfort of a Maybach in a body type of SUV selling like hotcakes in India. So, you get the practicality of an SUV without sacrificing the luxury features of a Spa. This SUV can also dance.

Mercedes-Benz Maybach GLS 600 4MATIC is the top model in the Mercedes-Maybach GLS  lineup and the price of the Maybach GLS top model is ₹ 2.80 Crore. It returns a certified mileage of 8.5 kmpl. This 600 4MATIC variant comes with an engine putting out 550 bhp @ 6000 rpm and 730 Nm @ 2500 rpm of max power and max torque respectively. Mercedes-Benz Maybach GLS 600 4MATIC is available in Automatic (TC) transmission and offered in 8 colors: Brilliant Blue, Obsidian Black, Cavansite Blue, Selenite Silver, Emerald Green, Mojave Silver, Iridium Silver, and Polar White (Non-Metallic).
