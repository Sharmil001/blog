Kartik Aaryan gets India’s first McLaren GT Worth 5Cr+Gifted By T-series Owner Bhushan Kumar

Bollywood actor Kartik Aaryan has become the first Person in India to own McLaren GT making his 2nd sports car in his car collection. After the great success of Film Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 Producer Bhushan Kumar celebrated great success by gifting him McLaren GT .

Kartik shared a couple of Pictures in social media where he is seen posing along with his new car With caption “Chinese khaane ke liye nayi table gift mil gayi 🍜🚗 Mehnat ka phal meetha hota hai suna tha..Itna bada hoga nahi pata tha ❤️ India’s 1st McLaren Gt 🥹 Agla gift Private jet sir 😂”.

McLaren claims that the GT has a top speed of 326.7 km/h (203 mph), it can accelerate from 0–97 km/h (0–60 mph) in 3.1 seconds, and 0–200 km/h (0–124 mph) in 9 seconds.

Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 has ended it's run at the box office with a worldwide gross of Rs 250 crore.
